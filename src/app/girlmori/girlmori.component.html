girlmori

Remember what they took from you...

A girl version of Omori was planned for the original game but was sadly cut due to time constraints, so I decided to add her back in! This mod replaces sprites, cutscenes, dialogue, animation, and more!

Development Process

Back in 2020, the indie game OMORI was released by OMOCAT and took the world by storm. I started this project out of love for the game and its community, and ended up collaborating with over fourteen talented programmers and artists. I managed the mod from start to finish and guided the team on artwork, animations, programming features, and more. I also hand-made a majority of the assets and even learned how to program to go into the code myself and change in-game events and dialogue. I wanted to catch every detail I could and put out the best mod possible.

As of 2025, the mod has over 4000 downloads and has been featured in many fan videos and other mods across the world.

Design Goals

The ultimate goal of the mod was to recreate the game with a girl protagonist as accurately as possible. This meant closely studying the art style of OMORI and hand-drawing/animating many of the assets. Assets that couldnâ€™t be hand-drawn were created via photomanipulation and collage in Photoshop.
